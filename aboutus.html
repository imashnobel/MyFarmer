About Us

BACKGROUND

As an agrarian country, Bangladesh depends heavily on the agricultural industry for its economic progress. According to the 2017 Economic Survey of Bangladesh, the agricultural sector contributes 14.2 percent to the gross domestic product (GDP).

About 70 percent people rely on agriculture for their livelihood. The role of capital input is becoming more and more important with the advancement of farm technology, however unfortunately most of the farmers in Bangladesh do not get adequate credit support.

To meet the challenge, we need to create opportunities so that smallholder farmers can develop their businesses and implement their concepts together with efforts to foster public confidence in the agricultural sector.

MyFarmer concentrates on assisting stakeholders in farming and agriculture to increase their out-turn and strengthen their profits with technology-driven services. MyFarmer opens opportunities for everyone to participate in agri-business through sponsoring in farming, which is beneficial for both farmers and general folks. We believe, together with MyFarmer, we can achieve an inclusive increase in living standards and economic progress in Bangladesh.


OUR MISSION

We are on a mission to enable everyone participate in the agricultural ecosystem through funding the capital requirement of the farmers and ensure global food security.

OUR VISION

To be the top-notch digital Agri-Tech platform in Bangladesh and empower farmers with technology and funds in building sustainable agribusinesses.


OUR SLOGAN

Fund Farm, Empower farmers & Earn Returns
